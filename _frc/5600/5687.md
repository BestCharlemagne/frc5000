Team 5687, **The Outliers**, is Baxter Academy's FIRST Robotics team from Portland, Maine.  
5687 strives to inspire teens to pursue engineering and technology through real life and hands on experiences. Members of The Outliers learn lifelong skills that will help them overcome challenges and achieve success in their postsecondary careers.
